### Data Quality Checklist

**Before collecting:**
- [ ] Clear definitions (what exactly am I measuring?)
- [ ] Consistent method (same way every time)
- [ ] Realistic schedule (can I sustain this?)
- [ ] Backup if I miss a day (how to handle?)

**During collection:**
- [ ] Record immediately (don't rely on memory)
- [ ] Check for errors (does this number make sense?)
- [ ] Track context (note unusual circumstances)
- [ ] Stay consistent (same time, same method)

**After collection:**
- [ ] Review all data (any obvious errors?)
- [ ] Calculate summary stats (mean, range, etc.)
- [ ] Spot check quality (random verification)
- [ ] Organize for analysis (spreadsheet or CSV)
